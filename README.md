# Silviu Pavelescu

Materials science researcher working on applied crystallography, DFT
simulations, and peer review for specialty journals. Based in Bucharest.

Currently editor-in-chief of the [Journal of Applied Matter Reviews](https://jamr-journal.online).

## Interests

- XRD pattern analysis, Rietveld refinement, peak deconvolution
- Ab-initio methods (Quantum ESPRESSO, VASP) for bulk and defect calculations
- Lattice reduction (Niggli/LLL) as preprocessing for DFT
- Peer review workflows and editorial tooling

## Selected works

- *Applied Matter Reviews*, 2024 — Editor-in-Chief and author of editorial
  style notes
- Peer review of ~30 manuscripts per year (anonymous)
- Internal tooling for the journal (XRD utilities, input generators)
